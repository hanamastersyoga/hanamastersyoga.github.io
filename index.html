About Me

I have been a vinyasa and yin yoga student since 2012. Originally self-taught, I got serious in 2017 when I began my practice with Bristol City Yoga, eventually becoming the regular help and assistance to the team there. Since then I have graduated from the 200 hour yoga teacher training course with a keen interest in body anatomy, chakras and mindful practice - and have started my own classes at Bristol Yoga Centre. I also volunteer at Elevate to help keep the space beautiful for all the classes.
